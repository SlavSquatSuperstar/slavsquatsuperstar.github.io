Video Game Soundtracks

Video games? Music? That's right—all those times you muted your games, so your mom wouldn't find out you were playing, were your loss. But it's not too late! Return to simpler times by browsing the OSTs (original soundtracks) below! If you're a new listener who needs a place to start, then give one of the albums below a listen. You surely won't be disappointed!
