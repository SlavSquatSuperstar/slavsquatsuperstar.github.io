Why Music?

From a young age, Comrade Kartoshkin was well-versed in classical music, taking piano lessons, singing in a choir, and playing in the school band. Along the way, a young Slav also discovered the magic of hardbass, which ignited a passion to seek new types of music. Thanks to the power of song, he transformed into the hardworking comrade he is today.

Nowdays, Slav spends his free time listening to video game soundtracks and singing along with old Soviet and Slavic songs.
