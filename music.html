Why Music?

From a young age, Comrade Kartoshkin was raised on a steady diet of classical music, taking piano lessons and playing in the school band. However, along the way, a lost young Slav discovered hardbass, which ignited a passion in his stagnant heart and transformed him into hardworking comrade he is today.

Nowdays, Slav spends his free time listening to video game soundtracks and singing along with old Russian and Soviet songs.
